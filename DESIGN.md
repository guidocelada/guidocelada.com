# Design System

## Theme

Light, poster-like personal brand surface inspired by social content graphics. A cool mist background carries bold black typography, with cyan and coral used as precise signals.

## Color Palette

- Background: `oklch(0.91 0.018 205)`
- Background cool: `oklch(0.87 0.055 195)`
- Ink: `oklch(0.12 0.015 230)`
- Muted ink: `oklch(0.37 0.035 225)`
- Cyan accent: `oklch(0.78 0.145 195)`
- Coral accent: `oklch(0.66 0.21 18)`
- White surface: `oklch(0.98 0.006 210 / 0.78)`

## Typography

Use the platform sans-serif stack. Headlines are heavy, compact, and tightly grouped without exceeding `-0.04em` letter spacing. Supporting text stays readable and conversational. Monospace appears only in faint technical atmosphere and compact metadata.

## Components

- Portrait: circular crop with a dark structural ring and offset cyan/coral signal marks.
- Primary button: cyan pill with dark text and a compact arrow.
- Secondary links: quiet text links that gain color and movement on interaction.
- Technical metadata: short monospace lines with low contrast and no interaction.

## Layout

Single-purpose hero centered in the viewport, with navigation and footer framing the composition. Mobile stacks all destinations; wider screens use a compact horizontal social row.

## Motion

Cursor movement shifts a soft background spotlight and attracts a sparse canvas network. The portrait and call-to-action use short ease-out transitions. Reduced-motion users receive a static background with no canvas animation.
