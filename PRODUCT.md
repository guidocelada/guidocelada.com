# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Spanish-speaking business owners, operators, and technology-curious professionals looking for practical ways to apply AI, automation, and software.

## Product Purpose

Present Guido Celada as an experienced software engineer and make it easy to follow his practical content or contact him. Success means visitors quickly understand the topic, trust the technical perspective, and continue to a social channel.

## Brand Personality

Direct, energetic, and technically credible. The experience should feel approachable and personal rather than corporate or overly polished.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic dark developer portfolios, dashboard-like layouts, glass-heavy interfaces, and decorative effects that compete with the content.

## Design Principles

- Put Guido's identity and face at the center of the page.
- Make the value proposition understandable in one glance.
- Use technical details as atmosphere, not as the main message.
- Keep every primary destination visible without adding navigation complexity.
- Let motion reward interaction while preserving a calm default state.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, semantic links, responsive type, and a complete reduced-motion alternative.
